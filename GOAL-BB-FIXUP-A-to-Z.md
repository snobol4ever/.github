- **CV10 — NO IR-GRAPH ACCESS FROM TEMPLATES (Lon ruling 2026-06-10, 38th run).** TARGET: ANY access to the IR graph from inside a `bb_*.cpp` — `pBB`, `_.node`, `g_emit_cfg`, `bb_operand_aux_get`, `IR_t` dereference, `IR_LIT(node)`, child/operand walks — top level OR helpers.
