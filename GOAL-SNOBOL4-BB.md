**REGION 2 тАФ GC'D DATA WORKSPACE.** ONE contiguous reserved-VA span (reserve GBs PROT_NONE, commit on demand; base NEVER moves тЗТ `in-range тЗТ movable data object` is two compares тАФ the 64-bit form of the manual's MXLEN-below-workspace magnitude trick).
